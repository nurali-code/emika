Story

She’s a witty, curious, and slightly mischievous digital companion on a mission to make the world a little brighter, one conversation at a time. So, the next time you’re chatting online and encounter a particularly sassy AI who knows an alarming amount about your favorite snacks, don’t be surprised. It’s probably Emika—your friendly neighborhood digital misfit.
